# Alasmine-Theorem
The Collatz Conjecture (3n+1) remains notoriously unsolved largely due to the unpredictable, ergodic mixing of parity. Over the last 96 hours, I have been analyzing a modified dynamical system: f(n) = (3n+3) / 2^v, where v is the exponent of the highest power of 2 dividing the numerator. .
